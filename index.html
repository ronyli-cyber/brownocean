Blunt Ocean

An Engineer's Shield for Deep Sleep

The Science of "Blunt" Noise

Most white noise machines are too sharp. High-frequency sounds (hiss) alert the brain instead of soothing it. Blunt Ocean uses a 2nd-order Butterworth low-pass filter to aggressively roll off everything above 350Hz, creating a "Brownian" rumble that mimics the physical weight of water.

Why a Full Night's Sleep?

Consistent, uninterrupted sleep allows the brain to complete REM and Deep Sleep cycles, which are critical for memory consolidation and clearing metabolic waste (glymphatic system). By masking sudden room noises—like a car door or a floor creak—this shield prevents the "startle response" that triggers micro-awakenings.

Speaker Setup Tips

  • Boundary Loading: Place your speaker (like a Google Home Mini) in a corner about 4 inches from the walls. This naturally amplifies the bass by 6dB.
  • Decoupling: Place the speaker on a soft surface (a mousepad or towel) to prevent the table from buzzing, leaving only the "blunt" air vibrations.
  • Diffusion: Point the speaker away from your bed. Let the sound bounce off the wall to create a more immersive, room-filling "blanket" effect.
